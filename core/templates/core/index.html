About Us

Learn more about us

eLearning Solutions Limited (ELS) is a Company leveraging on Digital Technology to design, deliver and deploy Education and training solutions across the spectrum that includes the youth, Institutions and Corporates. Our solutions range from custom content development, rapid authoring, mobile learning, responsive content, game-based learning, social learning solutions, translation and localization in various languages. We pride in offering our clients training packages that ensure optimal use of time available through a blended approach in both virtual and face time training. We offer consultancy in training on content design, graphic design, digital content authoring, online facilitation and online course management. We are a National, Industrial and Technical Authority (NITA) accredited eLearning provider. We have pioneered in high quality and cost-effective E-learning solutions in Education in Kenya. We have the experience, expertise and access to key resources that will enable our clientele to fulfill their online requirements.

Recruitment Collaboration

We collaborate with recruiters who are responsible for local and international job opportunities. Our goal is to ensure that new hires are well-prepared for their roles by providing them with our comprehensive 21st-century soft skills training.
